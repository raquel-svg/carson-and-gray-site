Why Carson & Gray

What we stand for.

i.

Personally Vetted

Every candidate is screened by Raquel personally. Not by a team. Not by an algorithm. Not by a junior recruiter following a script. You get her direct recommendation, backed by a real conversation, a real skills assessment, and real reference checks. If she wouldn't vouch for someone herself, they don't make the shortlist.

ii.

Ethically Priced

We charge clients a fair placement fee. We never take a cut from candidate salaries. The number agreed between the client and candidate is what the candidate receives. No hidden margin. We believe the cost advantage of South African talent should benefit both parties, not get absorbed by the agency in the middle.

iii.

Long-term Minded

We are not a staffing platform optimising for volume. We make careful, considered placements. We'd rather decline a brief than rush a shortlist. Our 90-day replacement guarantee reflects: if a placement doesn't work out within the first 90 days, we find a replacement at no additional cost. We back our work.
