Why South Africa

The talent advantage no one is talking about.

Four reasons our clients consistently outperform competitors who hire locally.

i

Timezone Overlap

GMT+2 in summer, GMT+1 in winter. Near-perfect alignment with UK and European business hours. US East Coast gets 4–6 hours of real-time overlap. No async-only arrangements — your team is actually present when you need them.

ii

English Fluency

South Africa has 11 official languages. English is the language of business, law, and commerce. Our candidates communicate clearly, professionally, and confidently in writing, on video, and in meetings — without the friction of language barriers.

iii

40% Cost Efficiency

Salaries in South Africa sit at roughly 40–60% below equivalent UK or US roles. Not because of lower quality — but because of purchasing power parity and currency differential. You get an excellent hire at a sustainable salary that's life-changing for them.

iv

Strong Work Culture

South African professionals are hungry for international exposure and take remote roles seriously. High ownership, strong communication, low turnover. Our clients consistently report that their SA hires outperform local equivalents in reliability and initiative.

Our process

How we find your next great hire.

i —

Intake Call

A focused 30-minute conversation to understand your business, the specific role, the culture fit you need, and the must-haves versus nice-to-haves. We take notes, ask hard questions, and leave with a clear brief. This is where most agencies skip steps — we don't.

ii —

Sourcing & Screening

We draw from our active database and headhunt passive candidates. Every applicant receives a structured screening call with Raquel, a CV scoring review, and role-specific skills assessment. Typically we screen 20–40 candidates to produce a shortlist of 3–5.

iii —

Shortlist Presentation

You receive a clean document: scored CVs, a 90-second Loom intro video from each candidate, our written notes on fit, and a recommended top pick. No filler. Every person on the shortlist is someone we would personally vouch for.

iv —

Placement & Guarantee

You choose your hire. We coordinate final interviews, facilitate the offer, and support the onboarding process. Every placement is backed by a 90-day replacement guarantee — if the hire doesn't work out within 90 days, we find a replacement at no additional charge.
