What to expect

Your journey from application to offer.

i —

Apply

Fill in the form below. Tell us about your experience, what you're looking for, and what makes you exceptional at what you do. Takes about 5 minutes. We read every application personally — no automated screening.

ii —

Screening Call with Raquel

If you're a strong fit, we'll book a 20–30 minute call. This isn't a formal interview — it's a conversation. We want to understand your goals, your strengths, and what kind of role and company you'd genuinely thrive in.

iii —

Vetting & Loom Intro

We'll ask you to record a short Loom intro video (90 seconds — no pressure, no production required). We'll also run a reference check and a role-relevant skills assessment. Once cleared, you join our active candidate pool with a complete profile.

iv —

Matched to Clients

When a client role matches your profile, we put you forward — always with your explicit permission. You'll know exactly what the role is before we share your details. We'll keep you updated throughout the process and give honest feedback either way.

What we look for

Who we work with.

We don't work with everyone. We work with professionals who are serious about remote work and ready for the responsibility that comes with international employment.

i —

5+ Years Experience

We place mid-to-senior professionals. You should have a clear track record in your field, and references who can speak confidently to the quality of your work.

ii —

Excellent Written English

Our clients are international. You need to communicate clearly and professionally in writing, on video calls, and in asynchronous tools like Slack, Notion, and email.

iii —

Reliable Setup

You need a stable internet connection, a quiet and professional workspace, and the tools to work effectively in a remote-first environment. We'll ask about your setup.

iv —

Long-term Mindset

Our clients want people who grow with the business — not short-term contractors or job-hoppers. We look for candidates who are ready to commit to the right role and stay.
