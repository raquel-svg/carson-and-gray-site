Recruitment built on relationships.

Carson & Gray was founded on a simple belief, that great recruitment is about people, not pipelines. We're a specialist remote recruitment agency placing exceptional South African professionals into agencies and SMBs across the US, UK, and Europe.

We don't chase numbers. We take time to understand both sides, vet every candidate ourselves, and make placements that last. That's how we work.

The hiring problem is getting harder.

Local salaries are rising faster than budgets. The right talent is harder to reach. We solve both.

01 —

Rising local costs

Local hires now cost 2-3x what they did five years ago.

Salaries in the US, UK and Europe have outpaced revenue growth for most SMBs. Hiring locally for every role is no longer realistic.

02 —

Generic outsourcing

The Philippines and India aren't always the right answer.

Timezone friction, cultural fit, and English fluency vary. South African talent solves all three for Western markets.

03 —

Slow time-to-hire

Generic agencies waste months presenting wrong candidates.

Specialist remote recruitment means faster shortlists, fewer wasted interviews, and roles filled within 14 days.

04 —

Poor retention

A bad hire costs more than an unfilled role.

We focus on long-term fit over volume. Every placement is backed by a 90-day replacement guarantee.

05 —

Hidden talent pool

South Africa's best are not on Upwork.

We source through trusted networks, referrals, and direct outreach. Most candidates we place have never been on a job board.

06 —

Unaligned incentives

Most recruiters get paid once. We're paid to stay involved.

Our 90-day guarantee means we're financially aligned with retention, not just placement.
